About JustEat

Founded in Denmark in 2001 by five entrepreneurs, Just Eat is a top 100 FTSE listed company with operations in 12 markets around the globe. Headquartered in London, we employ a team of over 3,300 talented employees.

Just Eat is a world leader in online and mobile food ordering, providing customers with an easy and secure way to order and pay for food from our restaurant partners.

We use our technology to bring together millions of customers with thousands of restaurant partners internationally, serving a diverse range of over 100 cuisine types - from grilled chicken, vegan and vegetarian options to traditional curries, burgers, sushi and pizza.
